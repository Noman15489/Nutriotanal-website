Diet Plan

A well-balanced diet plan can help you maintain a healthy weight, boost your energy levels, and promote overall well-being. Here’s a sample diet plan that focuses on wholesome, nutrient-dense foods and includes all the essential food groups.

Adjust portion sizes based on your activity level, age, and specific goals (e.g., weight loss, muscle building). If you have food allergies or intolerances, replace ingredients accordingly (e.g., use dairy-free yogurt or gluten-free grains). If you prefer plant-based options, replace animal proteins with plant-based alternatives like tempeh, lentils, or beans.
